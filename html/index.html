Official.

Our team is composed of nine students of the french engineering school ECE Paris. We came to this idea of an opensource and accessible robotics solution when we realized that the cheapest humanoïd robot kit cost more than £800,00. We looked for alternatives solutions but there was almost none of them. We are a group of motivated and skilful students with different skill sets which makes us stronger as a group.
